RAW file upload

State regulations require ambulance services to submit their Patient Care Reports (PCRs) to their regional council monthly.  The requirement is the same for paper and electronic PCR solutions.  EMSI provides this form as a way for ambulance services in the EMSI region to comply with this requirement.

Submissions from non-EMSI agencies will be rejected.  They will not be forwarded to any other regional council.
If your PCR solution requires you to export your trip sheet data to RAW files, and to submit those RAW files to EMSI, we strongly recommend you use this form to perform the submission.  This form not only accepts and forwards your RAW files appropriately -- it will also perform validation checks on the data inside your RAW file to reduce errant submissions. This form catches some errors immediately.  Other errors must be caught later.
You can submit one RAW file at a time using this form.

The burden to submit and maintain complete, valid, timely PCR data rests with the ambulance service.  Some electronic PCR solution providers may assume substantial portions of this burden from the ambulance service, but in such cases, it's up to the ambulance service to monitor the solution provider's compliance. Regular backups, including an off-site archiving scheme, are critical!  If your solution provider doesn't perform these services on your behalf, EMSI recommends you formalize your data recovery plan now!
