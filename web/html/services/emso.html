Services and Resources

for PA DOH EMSO

We help achieving a unified Statewide EMS system.
We collect and maintain standardized patient data and information.
We help ensure that the licensure, certification, recertification, recognition, registration and continuing education requirements of prehospital personnel and ambulance services are met.
We perform other duties for the initiation, expansion, maintenance and improvement of the regional and Statewide EMS system.
