Basic Recertification Requirements

To continue functioning at this level... ...you have this long... ...to complete this many total hours of con ed... ...of which at least this many hours must be in the Medical/Trauma category...
First Responder 3 years 16 8
EMT 3 years 24 12
Paramedic or PHRN* 1 year 18 9

*Technically, Pennsylvania paramedics and PHRNs are certified for life, but they must complete these requirements to become eligible to receive Medical Command Authorization.  An ALS practitioner who loses Medical Command Authorization must either initiate the Downgrade Process or stop practicing entirely.
