Technical help for prospective ePCR developers

The use of electronic patient care reporting forms in Pennsylvania's EMS system is subject to approval from the PA Dept of Health EMS Office (EMSO).  EMSO typically provides the following resources to vendors wishing to secure such approval for their product:

  • Semantic requirements and approval process

  • Paper PCR (non-electronic baseline)

  • Special codes (MCDs, hospitals, medical command facilities, etc)

  • Export specifications
