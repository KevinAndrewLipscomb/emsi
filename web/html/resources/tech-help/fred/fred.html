FRED

FRED is the Facility Resource Emergency Database, an Internet-based system that provides rapid, efficient, alert-driven reporting of resources available to mitigate mass casualty incidents or similar situations that produce a surge of patients.  It was originally developed by the Maryland Institute for EMS Systems and has since been adopted by the Pennsylvania Department of Health (PA DOH). Initial plans call for establishing Continuous FRED Monitoring Sites at all of the commonwealth's receiving hospitals, Public Safety Answering Points (PSAPs / 911 centers), Emergency Management Agencies, and emergency information centers such as Poison Centers.  FRED can also be accessed from any compliant Internet-connected web browser.

FRED's feature set is expected to grow as needed to fill its niche.  Because FRED is implemented as a web site, users will gain the benefit of any new features as they are released, without having to perform any special installations or upgrades.
