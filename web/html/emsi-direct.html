EMSI Direct yahoogroup

To promote efficient, direct collaboration among all practitioners in EMSI's region, EMSI has established the "EMSI Direct" yahoogroup.

When you join, you will receive occasional important notifications from EMSI. You can choose to receive these notifications by email, or you can visit the EMSI Direct archive to retrieve them at your convenience. You may unsubscribe at any time.

As a member of EMSI Direct, you can post messages to the group yourself. (Messages that do not originate from EMSI will be moderated.)

The EMSI Direct yahoo group also provides a full set of online collaboration tools, including chat, file and photo storage, favorite links list, database, polls, and calendar.

Stay current by getting state and regional EMS information directly from the regional council. Please join EMSI Direct today!
