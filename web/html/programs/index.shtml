Programs

  • Drill Reimbursement
    We verify participation in, and process reimbursement applications for, certain tabletop and field exercises.
     
  • EMSOF
    We distribute money that the state allocates to our region from the Emergency Medical Service Operating Fund.
     
  • Quality Improvement
    We develop and implement a regional EMS quality improvement program to monitor the delivery of EMS.
     
  • Rural AED Grant Program
    We distribute RAED money to successful applicants in our region.
