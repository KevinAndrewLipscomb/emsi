EMSI-QI yahoogroup

To promote efficient, direct collaboration among EMSI's Quality Leadership Working Group, EMSI has established the "EMSI-QI" yahoogroup.

Approved applicants will be part of the email dialog of EMSI's Quality Leadership Working Group.  You can choose to receive these emails directly, or in digest form, or you can visit the EMSI-QI archive to retrieve them at your convenience. You may unsubscribe at any time.

As a member of EMSI-QI, you can post messages to the group yourself.

The EMSI-QI yahoo group also provides a full set of online collaboration tools, including chat, file and photo storage, favorite links list, database, polls, and calendar.
