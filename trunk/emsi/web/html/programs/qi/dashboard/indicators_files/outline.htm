6
Caveats
  • Trend indications are hypotheses only.  Simple linear trend hypotheses are included on all charts.


  • If the phrase "There is poor evidence of a trend!!" appears on a chart, the statistical tests applied by the charting tool indicate low confidence in the validity of the trend hypothesis.


  • The more "Special Cause Flags" on a chart, the less confidence one should have in the simple linear trend hypothesis that is indicated.


  • Data points in the last quarter of 2003 are not reliable -- some EMSCAN submissions from that period are still outstanding.  All other data points are reliable.


  • The charts only include what is documented on EMSCAN records received by EMSI.
