EMSOF

The Emergency Medical Service Operating Fund (EMSOF) accumulates money from fines imposed on certain traffic violations, and is used for the initiation, expansion, maintenance and improvement of emergency medical services.

Background

EMSOF is established and affected by various sections (right) of the Pennsylvania EMS Act.
