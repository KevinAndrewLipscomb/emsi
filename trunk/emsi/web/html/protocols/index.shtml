Basic Life Support (BLS) ►

All services in the region are held to the Pennsylvania Statewide BLS Protocols.  Along with these protocols, the commonwealth also provides Guidelines that the region or any of its services may adopt as protocol.

Advanced Life Support (ALS) ►

In the EMSI region, ALS protocols are developed and maintained by the Medical Command Facilities (MCFs).  Each ALS Ambulance Service is held to the ALS protocols of its chosen MCF.

EMSI is happy to provide copies of, or links to, an MCF's ALS protocols whenever possible.
