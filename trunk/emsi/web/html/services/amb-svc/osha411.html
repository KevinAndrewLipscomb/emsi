OSHA411 yahoogroup

This public yahoogroup is a forum for the exchange of information regarding OSHA activity affecting Pennsylvania EMS agencies.

As of May of 2003, such OSHA activity has drawn attention from several stakeholders. It is evident that stakeholders in reactive and proactive stances are encountering a dearth of useful, authoritative, "actionable" information. This yahoogroup was created to support fast-cycle collaboration on the topic.

It's anticipated that this group will expand rapidly upon creation; that authoritative information will be discovered, shared, archived, compiled, and converted into FAQ form; that a critical mass of topical awareness will eventually be achieved; that the need for this yahoogroup will thereafter diminish, and the yahoogroup will be deactivated.

Topics include, but are not limited to: Where is OSHA conducting inspections? How are they choosing their "targets"? What are they looking for? Do their requirements meet the "sanity test"? If not, what can we do? If so, how can we achieve compliance? Etc, etc.

The yahoogroup was established and is moderated by Kevin Lipscomb, Assistant Executive Director, EMSI, as a way of efficiently managing the administrative load generated by the high degree of interest in the topic. Unless otherwise noted, EMSI does not have any official position regarding this forum's subject matter.
