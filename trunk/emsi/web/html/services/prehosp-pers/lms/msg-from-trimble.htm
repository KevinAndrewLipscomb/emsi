Welcome to Pennsylvania Learning Management System!

 

Thank you for your interest in the Learning Management System (LMS).  The LMS makes education and information available to you about critical public health and other emergency preparedness topics on demand.  There is no cost for the courses or the record keeping.

 

The Department of Health provides this support to you as part of its commitment to a prepared workforce.  A prepared workforce is an essential component in strengthening our Commonwealth’s public health system and for enhancing our ability to respond to incidents of bioterrorism, outbreaks of infectious disease, and other public health threats and emergencies.  The Learning Management System (LMS) contains courses and content from national and state experts.

 

Benefits to you include:

(1)   Access to the LMS 24 hours a day 7 days a week;

(2)   Access to LMS via any computer with Internet access at www.pahealthinfo.net;

(3)   Access to the most current information available at the time.  

 

Continuing education credits for courses is in the planning stage.  However, it is to your benefit to begin using the LMS immediately for:

(1)   Critical protection information;

(2)   In-depth announcements;

(3)   Information regarding threat agents;

(4)   Expert guidance on planning and response.

You have been logged on to the LMS.  You can expect to receive a follow up email from EMSED.com within three business days that will provide a unique user id for you, an initial password, and instructions on how to access the system and get started.  The LMS is administered by the Pennsylvania Department of Health.  The EMS Office liaison is Gail Dubs.  Any questions or concerns regarding LMS can be addressed to Gail Dubs at sdubs@state.pa.us

 

 

Thank you,
