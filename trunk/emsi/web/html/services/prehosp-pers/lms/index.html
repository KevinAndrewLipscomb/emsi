NOTE

When you complete a con-ed course on www.PaHealthInfo.net, the system will give you a completion certificate.  You can find your completion certificate in the PROFILE / My Certificates area. You must fax the completion certificate to your regional EMS council to get credit for taking the course.

In the EMSI region, you must also fax us a cover sheet with the following information:

  1. Your medical certification number
  2. Your medical certification level
  3. Your medical certification expiration date
  4. Your date of birth

EMSI's fax number is shown at the top of this page.

If you reside outside the EMSI region, fax the information to your local regional council.

 

Here's some terminology you should know, especially if you call for help:

LMS
Learning Management System.  A generic name for any self-paced instructional web site.
 
PaHealthInfo.net
The web address of the LMS provided by the Pennsylvania Department of Health.
 
EMSEd
The company that the PA DOH has hired to run PaHealthInfo.net.
 
CentreLearn
The technology that EMSed has chosen to implement PaHealthInfo.net.
