Reciprocity ("Certification By Endorsement")

» In accordance with Section V of the Pennsylvania Department of Health EMS Office Prehospital Personnel Manual (right), we process Certification By Endorsement ("Reciprocity") applications for the following Pennsylvania certifications:
  • First Responder
  • EMT
  • EMT-Paramedic
  • EMT-Instructor

To begin the process, contact the EMSI Reciprocity Coordinator (right).

NOTE:  Parts of this process cannot be conducted online.  For instance, the Student Application form must be completed with a Number 2 pencil, and must be fed manually into an OpScan™ machine.  Where appropriate, we have provided other parts online so that you can start completing them now.

The definitive way to get this process rolling is to contact the EMSI Reciprocity Coordinator.
