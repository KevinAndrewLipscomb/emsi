Con Ed

"Con Ed" accounts for the vast majority of phone calls, web hits, and site searches received by EMSI.  Here's what our constituents want to know most often:

How many Con Ed hours must I get?

Please see our basic recertification requirements page.

How many Con Ed hours do I have on file?

You can check this yourself using EMSAMS.

When and where are Con Ed classes being offered?

This information is also available at EMSAMS.

I need help using EMSAMS.

Please seek assistance from these people, in this order:

  • Your service's medical training officer
  • Other officers at your service, according to your service's SOPs
  • Your County Training Coordinator
  • EMSI

Can I take Con Ed classes ONLINE?

Yes.  See our Web-based Con Ed page.

What other kinds of Con Ed are available?

  • Journal-based con ed
  • Videotape-based con ed

My Con Ed report contains an error.  How do I get it fixed?

Please see our page on resolving Con Ed Discrepancies.

How can I get credit for a class that is not pre-approved in Pennsylvania?

Submit a completed Continuing Education By Endorsement form along with the materials requested therein.

My EMT certification expired.  What do I do now?

Please see our page on the reinstatement process.

Military duty prevented me from renewing my certification or medical command authorization.  How can I renew?

See the commonwealth's policy on recertification / reauthorization for active duty military
